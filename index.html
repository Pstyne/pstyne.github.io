Mission

The mission of Colonel's Kids is to help in crimes against children and to prevent crimes from being commited against children.

All children deserve a memorable childhood. Memories they develop that they can then share with their children someday and pave the way to guiding and caring for their own. Unfortunately, many children suffer from the opposite of that childhood. Living lives that are unimaginable. Help us, help the wonderful children.

Who we are

We are Colonels Kids. A non-profit organization based in Fayetteville, West Virginia. For some time, we have put forth much effort in stopping and preventing crimes against children. We are also a family and we take wholeness of a family seriously.

Tim Pack, President

Tim Pack is the president of Colonels Kids. Making it a top priority that the organizations needs are met and making sure children are given top priority in the mission to stop crimes against children.

Teresa Pack, Secretary Treasurer

Teresa Pack is the secretary treasurer of Colonels Kids. She keeps track of the organizations finances and makes sure that the top priority of the organization is met and keeping financial balance in the organization.
